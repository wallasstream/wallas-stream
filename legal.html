Legal Information — Wallas’ Stream

Last updated: January 2026

Wallas’ Stream consists of:
• Wallas’ Stream for macOS
• Wallas’ Stream for iPhone (iOS)
• Wallas Audio, a virtual audio device for macOS

Wallas’ Stream is developed and operated by Wallas. It is designed to work locally on your devices without collecting personal data.

Privacy Policy

1. Overview

  • requires no user accounts
  • uses no cloud storage
  • sends no personal data to external servers

All processing happens locally and only during active use. No background recording or continuous listening occurs when the App is not actively in use.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • names, emails, or phone numbers
  • contacts
  • location data
  • audio recordings
  • voice or biometric data
  • advertising identifiers
  • user tracking or analytics linked to identity

3. Wallas Audio (macOS Virtual Audio Device)

  • routes system audio in real time
  • does not record, store, or analyze audio
  • does not transmit data externally
  • runs only when explicitly selected by the user

4. Audio Handling

macOS App

  • Audio is transmitted live and temporarily
  • Audio is never stored
  • The user manually selects the audio output device
  • The App does not automatically change system audio outputs
  • No audio persists after the session ends

Transmission can occur via:

  • local network (Wi-Fi)
  • direct wired connection (USB cable)

iPhone App

  • Receives audio only
  • Plays audio in real time
  • Never records or stores audio
  • Audio never leaves the device

5. Permissions Used

macOS may request:

  • Audio / Microphone access
    Required by macOS for real-time audio routing. This permission is not used for recording.
  • Local Network access
    Used to connect directly to your Mac on the same network.

iPhone (iOS) may request:

  • Camera
    Used only to scan QR codes to establish a connection.
  • Bluetooth
    Used only to connect to user-approved external audio or hearing devices.
  • Local Network
    Used for direct audio transmission from your Mac.
  • Notifications (Optional)
    Used only to alert you when the connection is disconnected due to prolonged silence or inactivity. You can use the App without enabling notifications.

6. Local Data Stored on iPhone

  • first use date
  • listening time today
  • listening time this week
  • total listening time

This data never leaves your device, is not linked to your identity, and is not shared with Wallas or any third party.

7. Notifications

  • do not contain personal data
  • are not used for tracking, marketing, or advertising

8. Widgets, Live Activities, and Dynamic Island

  • display only local connection status and listening time
  • use data stored locally on the device
  • do not transmit any data to external servers
  • do not collect personal or identifying information

9. Third-Party Services

  • uses no advertising SDKs
  • uses no third-party analytics
  • integrates no tracking frameworks

10. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Because no personal data is collected, no child data is stored.

11. Data Security

Since no personal data is transmitted or stored externally, exposure risk is minimal. Connections are temporary, local, and initiated by the user.
